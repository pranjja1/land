About Us

  • Our Mission

    At DGBOXOFFICE, we aim to empower creators by providing a platform to showcase and monetize their talent without any barriers.

  • Our Vision

    To become the go-to platform for independent content creators worldwide, fostering creativity and innovation

  • Our Team

    Founder & CEO DGBOXOFFICE LLP

    Ashish has a career spanning over 25 years in the Technology and Media & Entertainment Industry. He is an Avid Independent Film Maker, Educationist, Computer Scientist, Pioneer in Digital Cinematography, Post Production, Digital Cinema and Video Streaming. He has worked with the biggest companies in the business Zee Group, Digital Academy, UFO Movies, Prime Focus, Sony Group, Ultra Media and Entertainment, Carnival Cinemas, Zee5 to make a few. He feels strongly for the Independent Film Makers and their struggles and wants to give them a platform where they can release their content with minimum hassle and make maximum money. Additionally he wants to give the Indian Content Creators a Platform to directly serve their Viewers for a fee and make maximum revenue without sharing it with the current platforms. This has become his Mission and is DGBOXOFFICE.

    Co - Founder & CEO DGBOXOFFICE LLP

    He is a Veteran Engineer who has served the TATA Group in major industries like Heavy Engineering, Data Centres, Cloud Computing and Telecom over a career spanning 30 years. He had setup one of the most popular Video Post Production Studios in Mumbai as early as the 80s and has been connected with the Media and Entertainment Industry since then. He is the Owner of Broadcasting Services, the preferred Data Centres Infrastructure Design and Development Company in India. He has joined the Mission DGBOXOFFICE with his years of invaluable experience as a Partner and strives to give Independent Film Makers their Due on this Platform.
